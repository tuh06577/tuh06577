Sharing Stories, Telling Tales About Helen

Ever since I wrote my first published story, I knew that the world of words would be my future. Granted, the story debuted on my fridge and featured a flying cat as the main character, but it still got raving reviews from my parents. Today, I advocate for stories, albeit more important ones than animals with superpowers, as a student in Temple University's Master of Public Policy program. As I continue to grow as a policy advocate, I aim to expand my knowledge of program development, GIS programming, and problem solving. In the future, I will use my literary passions, combined with the strategic data skills, to find harmony between the arts, education, and data for the betterment of our global communities.
