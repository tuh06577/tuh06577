Tourism and Crime Airbnb Review Distribution

Tourism is affected by many variables, including the perceived safety of a location. This map shows the spatial distribution of Airbnb reviews in Chicago based on the cityâ€™s police districts. The choropleth design of the map shows that Airbnbs are concentrated in the northeast corner of the city, with rates decreasing for outside police districts. This information could be used by the Chicago Police Department to allocate resources to areas with the most Airbnbs to address concerns about decreasing tourism. This map required skills of creating choropleth maps, utilizing visual hierarchy, changing coordinate reference systems, and finding relevant data via selection tools. To utilize data on both police districts and Airbnb rentals, I used new skills of joining tables, summarizing data based on functions and grouping fields, and creating new fields of data. The ability to create new fields allowed me to expand on existing data by adding accurate area calculations and density calculations.

The Power of Simplicity Flint Water Crisis

This map represents the ongoing water crisis in Flint, Michigan. In the heart of the city, selected red areas show where three water risk factors are all present: rivers, lead service pipes, and facilities that produce over 10,000 tons of pollution. The scale shows the long rivers that bisect the city, presenting challenges to control water pollution. These high-risk areas spread across the center of the city, showing that the crisis is far from over. This project was a cumulation of my skills; I needed to join different data tables, calculate geometry, and display selected subsets of information. Clipping the rivers to the boundaries of the Flint wards allows the visual focus to remain on the communities most affected in Flint. The simplicity of the final design required numerous steps to consolidate and calculate data.

Political Power Pennsylvania Election Map

Traditionally a swing state and decider of presidencies, this Pennsylvania election map illustrates the divisions between north and south, urban and rural, and decisive and uncertain. Democratic strongholds, such as Philadelphia and Pittsburgh are deeply blue, while outlying Republican areas flash deeply red. Between saturated colors, much of the state is shaded indecisive lighter shades of baby blue and faint red. These areas show opportunities for political intervention or areas for improvement for parties losing vote share. This map presented the unique challenge of scaling the choropleth color spectrum to match the relative number of votes. This project required combining data files, calculating fields, and careful color selection to match election map standards. In addition to color recognition, the coordinate reference system needed to be adjusted to accurately represent Pennsylvania, maintaining area and shape proportions.
